About Me

Hi! Welcome to my website! I'm a 2nd-year postgraduate student at Peking University. Prior to this, I completed my Bachelor’s degree at Xiamen University. I am preparing for my PHD application and my research interests include Human-Computer Interaction, Accessibility, and Mixed Reality. I am keen on studying how to improve the form of interaction between humans and technology, so as to achieve a more natural, efficient, and inclusive human-computer interaction. Please feel free to contact me if you share common research interests.
